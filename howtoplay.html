How to play rugby

Rugby seems like an complex sport when you watch it however it is simple once you learn it. To play you must have:

A ball
15 people per team
A field
Cleats and a mouthguard

The most important rules

  • Must pass backwards
  • Must tackle below the shoulders
  • Dropping the ball forwards is a knock on and calls for a scrum
  • Going out of bounds calls for a lineout
